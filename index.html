Hi there !

I am an astronomer (recently graduated!) at the Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics of the Australian National University in Canberra, under the supervision of Prof. Michael Dopita. My research addresses the physics of star formation in interacting galaxies located in compact groups, which I study via optical integral field spectroscopy. And that is very much a work in progress !

I will soon become a fellow of the European Southern Observatory (ESO) in Santiago (Chile) starting December 1st, 2015.

You can get a better idea of what I do in the Science section, or directly take a look at my scientific publications on the SAO/NASA-ADS database: either ranked by publication date, or by citation counts.

As one would expect, these pages will evolve as time and results (!) permit ...

Cheers !
