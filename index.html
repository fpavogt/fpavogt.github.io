Hi there !

I am a Fellow at the European Southern Observatory since December 2015, with duties at the Unit Telescope 4 (UT4) of ESO's Very Large Telescope (VLT) on Cerro Paranal. My scientific research primarily addresses the physics of star formation in galaxies, which I study via optical integral field spectroscopy. I also spend some of my time working on oxygen-rich young supernova remnants in the Magellanic Clouds and our own Galaxy.

The easiest way to get a better idea of what I do is probably to take a look at my scientific publications on the SAO/NASA-ADS database: either ranked by publication date, or by citation counts.
