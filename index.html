Hi there !

I am a Scientific Collaborator at the Federal Office of Meteorology and Climatology MeteoSwiss in Payerne (Switzerland) since February 2020.

I started my scientific career with a PhD in Astronomy and Astrophysics at the Australian National University, followed by a Fellowship at the European Southern Observatory in Chile. Back then, my scientific research was primarily dedicated to the observation of young supernova remnants using optical integral field spectroscopy. The easiest way to get a better idea of what I did in those years is probably to take a look at my scientific publications on the SAO/NASA-ADS database, using:
